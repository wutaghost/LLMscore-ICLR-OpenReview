009                                               A BSTRACT
010
011
               Neural network PDE solvers have recently gained popularity.However, it faces
012
               difficulty to deal with sharp discontinuity like shock waves in hyperbolic conser-
013
               vation laws.In this paper we propose a characteristic-based neural network to solve
014            one dimension hyperbolic laws.The smooth solution can be derived by equation
015            of characteristic lines ,and shock waves are decided by simple ODE solver.This
016            method achieves a high accuracy with high efficiency. In the future it is hopeful
017            to apply this method to higher dimension problems.

      1    I NTRODUCTION
022
023   Hyperbolic conservation law is one of the important equations that represent fluid conservation
024   law . It is widely used in fluid mechanics, electromagnetic field theory and other fields,including
025   convection equation, Burger’s equation, Euler equations, etc. For nonlinear hyperbolic conservation
026   law equations, even smooth the initial value could result in complex local discontinuities . Therefore,
027
      more special numerical methods are required to obtain high-precision approximate solutions.
028   Neural networks have been widely utilized as PDE solver due to its excellent capacity of approxima-
029   tion . One popular example is physics-informed neural network(PINN)Raissi et al. (2019) , which
030   approximates the PDE with neural network and utilizes auto-differentiation to construct loss func-
031   tion.The result of PINN does not guarantee physical nature ,therefore more targeted methods incor-
032   porating physics into problem formulating are developed.MüllerMüller (2023) constructs a neural
033
      network with built-in symmetry that preserves the Lagrangian exactly.The prediction of trajecto-
      ries is more reliable due to the inherent conservation of network.Other approaches attempt to com-
034
      bine machine learning with traditional numerical methods like finite point methodMorand et al.
035
      (2024)Chen et al. (2024), discontinuous Galerkin methodArora (2023),WENO Wang et al. (2020).
036
037   In fact, due to the discontinuities in hyperbolic conservation systems , it is challenging to directly
038   apply neural network to approximate solution,which will lead to paradoxical results and consider-
039
      able error. To tackle this issue, some novel methods have been developed.Conservative physics-
      informed neural network (cPINN) Jagtap et al. (2020)divides the original domain into small sub-
040
      domains and apply PINN on each of them.To obtain conservation , constraints of flux on the in-
041
      terface of subdomains are added to the overall loss function .Compared to PINN, cPINN performs
042   better on complex-shaped domains and equations with discontinuous solutions.Li et al.Liu et al.
043   (2023) proposed PINNs-WE(Physics-Informed Neural Networks with Equation Weight) to identify
044   the location of shock waves and adjust the loss function dynamically during the training. The Rank-
045   ine–Hugoniot (RH) relation is also incorporated to obtain conservation at discontinuous region.
046   NDNN(Non-Diffusive Neural Network)Lorin & Novruzi (2024) represent the position of discon-
047   tinuity lines by neural networks and decompose the global space-time region accordingly. This
048   method is capable of calculating complex phenomena like shock wave interaction and shock wave
049   generation in one dimensional hyperbolic conservation law.
050   The researches mentioned above adopt different approaches of establishing neural networks to tackle
051   the discontinuities , but all of them require formulation of complex loss functions (NDNN, cPINN)
052   or intricate tuning of parameters Liu et al. (2023) ,and the convergence is hard to guarantee under
053   complex circumstances like multiple shock waves.In fact, the features of hyperbolic conservation
      law have not been fully explored .

054
      In this paper we propose a novel characteristic-based neural network(CBNN) to solve hyperbolic
055   conservation laws.In this method,the space-time region is divided into subdomains each influenced
056   by a part of the initial value function , and the solution on each subdomain is easily obtained us-
057   ing characteristic lines.The shock wave is determined afterwards by solving the ODE of R-H rela-
058   tion.The training process of each process is independent and convenient,and the shock wave is easily
059   derived by ode solver like Euler method. This method is robust and efficient dealing with diverse
060   type of discontinuities.
061   In section 2 we will introduce some preliminaries of one-dimensional hyperbolic conservation
062   laws,and apply CBNN to different type of discontinuities like shock wave interaction and shock
063   wave generation.Section 3 gives a theoretical proof of convergence and some numerical experiments
064   are conducted in section 4.Finally a brief conclusion is in section 5.

285   5    C ONCLUSION
286
287   In this paper, we propose a new method to solve one dimension hyperbolic conservation laws.This
288   characteristic-based neural network applies the rule of characteristic lines and guarantees conver-
289   gence of solution directly.Complex local discontinuities like shock waves can be derived by R-H
290   relation.This method will be applied to higher dimension and hyperbolic systems in the future.
