Release safety scan summary
===========================

Date: 2026-05-28
Dataset: LLMscore-ICLR-OpenReview-Link

Checks
- No paper PDFs are redistributed.
- No extracted paper full text is redistributed.
- Reviewer identifiers are anonymized within paper.
- Released data files are compressed JSONL only.
- ICLR 2023, 2024, and 2025 data are stored in separate yearly directories.
- OpenReview forum and PDF links are preserved as source references.
- Anchor scoring is based on a unified ChatGPT-4o protocol across all yearly subsets.
- Candidate papers were filtered using a ChatGPT-4o familiarity check to reduce potential data leakage risk.

Notes
- This release is intended for peer review analysis in the context of the paper
  "Position: Peer Review Should Be Calibrated via LLM Scoring".
